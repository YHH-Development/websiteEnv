About Our NGO

Young Helping Hands is a non-governmental organization (N.G.O.) which is registered under Society Act with Uttar Pradesh Govt. Young Helping Hands driven by Crowd Funding. We are dedicated to creating positive and sustainable change in society. With a strong focus on addressing essential needs, we work tirelessly to uplift individuals and communities through various initiatives such as food donation, cloth donation, animal care, provision of basic necessities to the poor, sanitary napkin donation, and basic education.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and uplift individuals and communities by addressing essential needs through food donation, cloth donation, animal care, provision of basic necessities to the poor, sanitary napkin donation, and basic education. We aim to create a more equitable society where everyone has access to the resources and opportunities necessary for a dignified and fulfilling life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a world where no one goes hungry, where basic necessities are readily available to all, and where education and compassion shape a brighter future for every individual. We envision communities where animals are treated with care and respect, where women's health and well-being are prioritized, and where poverty is overcome through sustainable solutions.
