Young Helping Hands

Young Helping Hands is a non-governmental organization (N.G.O.) which is registered under Society Act with Uttar Pradesh Govt. Young Helping Hands driven by Crowd Funding. We are dedicated to creating positive and sustainable change in society. With a strong focus on addressing essential needs, we work tirelessly to uplift individuals and communities through various initiatives such as food donation, cloth donation, animal care, provision of basic necessities to the poor, sanitary napkin donation, and basic education.

Our Priorities

Food Donation:


We firmly believe that no one should go to bed hungry. Our food donation program aims to tackle hunger and food insecurity by collecting surplus food from various sources and distributing it to those in need.


Cloth Donation:


Another aspect of our work focuses on cloth donation. We collect gently used or new clothing items and distribute them to those who lack adequate clothing. Our goal is to promote dignity, warmth, and protection by providing appropriate clothing to individuals, including children, adults, and the elderly.

Sanitary Napkin Donation:


Our commitment to women's health and empowerment is evident through our sanitary napkin donation program. We recognize the importance of menstrual hygiene and strive to ensure that every woman and girl has access to sanitary napkins


Animal Care:


At Empowering Lives, Enriching Communities, we recognize the importance of animal welfare. We extend our compassion and care to animals by supporting local animal shelters and rescue organizations. Through our initiatives, we provide resources, veterinary care, and awareness programs to ensure the well-being and protection of animals in our communities.
