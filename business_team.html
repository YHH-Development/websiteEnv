About Us

Young Helping Hands is a non-governmental organization (N.G.O.) which is registered under Society Act with Uttar Pradesh Govt. Young Helping Hands driven by Crowd Funding. We are dedicated to creating positive and sustainable change in society.

With a strong focus on addressing essential needs, we work tirelessly to uplift individuals and communities through various initiatives such as food donation, cloth donation, animal care, provision of basic necessities to the poor, sanitary napkin donation, and basic education.
